Air Jordan 1 OG LOW 'Black Toe'

The Air Jordan 1 Low 'Black Toe' brings an OG Air Jordan 1 colorway to a low-cut iteration. The shoe's upper is built with Gym Red leather on the heel and collar and white leather on quarter panel and perforated toe box. The Swoosh, eyestay and toe box are finished in black nubuck, while the 'Wings' logo has been debossed tonally on the collar. Underfoot, the white midsole houses Air for cushioning.
