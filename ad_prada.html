PRADA X SUPERSTAR 'WHITE BLACK'

The Prada x Superstar 'White Black' & Bag Bundle released alongside a matching leather bag. Limited to 700 pairs and released in 2019 for the 50th anniversary of the silhouette, the shoe emerges in Prada's Spazzolato Rois leather, built in Italy and finished entirely in white. The lateral side includes contrasted black Prada branding, while the signature shelltoe leads into a classic rubber cupsole underfoot, sporting a herringbone pattern for traction.
