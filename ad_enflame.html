YEEZY BOOST 700 'ENFLAME AMBER'

The Yeezy Boost 700 'Enflame Amber' features a contrasting look between its upper and tooling. The shoe's upper is built with mesh, overlaid by suede and finished in a mix of brown and beige. A tonal toe cap supports the fit, while a hit of blue marks the mesh toe box itself. Underfoot, drop-in Boost provides cushioning, overlaid by an orange EVA carrier, while the herringbone rubber outsole reveals the cushioning unit itself via cutout detailing.
