AIR JORDAN 4 RETRO GS 'WHITE OREO'

The Air Jordan 4 Retro GS 'White Oreo' is a grade-school iteration of the shoe known for 'The Shot.' Appearing here in a neutral colorway, the shoe's upper is built with tumbled leather, finished in white and complemented by Tech Grey throughout, including on the speckled, molded eyelets. The usual mesh inserts mark the quarter panel and vamp, while the tongue adds Fire Red via the Jumpman branding. Underfoot, visible Air in the heel of the midsole provides cushioning.
