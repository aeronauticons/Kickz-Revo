PHARRELL X NMD HUMAN RACE 'YELLOW'

Pharrell Williams teamed up with adidas to create the Pharrell x NMD Human Race 'Yellow.' The shoe emerges with a largely monochromatic look, finished in yellow throughout its Primeknit construction. Wraparound laces are worked into inserts at the midfoot, while Hindi characters down the vamp read 'Human Race.' The inner lining contrasts the look in black, while underfoot, the full-length Boost midsole includes plugs at the forefoot and heel for stability.
